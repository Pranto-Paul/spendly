Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Spendly is built around your financial privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and a hashed password. When you use Spendly, we store the expense entries you create — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate the service: to display your expenses, generate summaries, and keep your account in sync across sessions. We do not use your financial data for advertising.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only with the infrastructure providers that host Spendly, and only the minimum needed to run the service.

4. Cookies & Sessions

Spendly uses session cookies to keep you signed in. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that follow you across other sites.

5. Data Security

Passwords are stored using modern hashing algorithms. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. While no system is perfectly secure, we work continuously to protect your information.

6. Your Rights

You can view, edit, or delete any of your expense records at any time. You can request a full export of your data, or request account deletion, from your profile settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We will post any updates to this policy on this page and update the date above. Material changes will also be announced in-app.

8. Contact

Privacy questions? Email us at privacy@spendly.app.
